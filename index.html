About Me

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin.
I'm particularly interested in networking, self-hosting, and infrastructure automation. To support my learning, I built a homelab environment where I test and explore various technologies hands-on.
I document everything I work on to make troubleshooting and future setups easier. You can find my documentation and side projects below.


Current Homelab

I'm using Proxmox VE as the hypervisor for all my virtualized services. For storage, I use TrueNAS Scale, taking advantage of ZFS for snapshots and backups.
Networking is handled with pfSense as the firewall/router, along with a combination of TP-Link and MikroTik devices for switching and routing.
Most of my self-hosted services are deployed using Docker Compose, with data stored on NFS volumes. Services are reverse proxied internally for access.
